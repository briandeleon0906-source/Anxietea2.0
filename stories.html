Small Wins Matter

Today I did something that might seem small to others, but it was huge for me. Ive done my speech without having a panic attack. This might not sound like much, but for someone who's havinganxiety, it's everything.

I've been working with my therapist on exposure therapy, and it's been challenging. But moments like today remind me why it's worth it. The fear doesn't disappear, but I'm learning to live alongside it.

If you're reading this and feeling stuck, know that small steps count. You don't have to do everything at once. Celebrate the small wins - they add up to big changes over time.

Thank you to everyone. Your courage inspires me to keep going.
